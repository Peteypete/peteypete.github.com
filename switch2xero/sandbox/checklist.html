Checklist of items to consider before submitting your .qbw or .qbb file

 

                  Necessary Conditions for conversion

  • Xero allows for only 1 Accounts Payable and 1 Accounts Receivable account.  Any extra AP or AR accounts including inactive accounts must be consolidated and/or deleted.
  •  The file must not contain Inventory assemblies, including inactives; currently we do not support conversion of these.
  •  The file must not have Multi-currency transactions turned on. You can check this in File -> Preferences -> Multiple Currencies.
  •  Currently we do not offer conversion of QuickBooks Enterprise edition.
  •  If you are converting from QuickBooks online you must first convert your QBO data to QB Desktop. A guide for converting to Desktop can be found at http://support.quickbooks.intuit.com/support/articles/HOW12246.
  •  If you are converting from QuickBooks Macintosh you must first select  File -> ‘back up to Quickbooks for Windows’, and submit the Windows .qbb file. 

 

                  Other items to consider

  • - A new client company will be created in Xero for you so there is no need to create an org prior to conversion.
  • - It’s ideal to submit a fully reconciled QB file.
  • - General Journal Entries to Accounts Payable and Accounts Receivable are not permitted in Xero. If you have entered journals to these accounts please be aware that in the resulting Xero file the trial balances will match the Quickbooks file, but the Open AP and AR balances will not.  AP or AR Items that were cleared in Quickbooks with gerenal journal entries will remain open in Xero.
  • - Currently the conversion process does not convert Classes in Quickbooks to Tracking Codes in Xero.
  • - Make sure account numbers are turned on in QuickBooks. This is done in Quickbooks preferences.  Review your chart of accounts in Quickbooks and assign account numbers if any are missing. Xero requires account number (up to 10 characters) otherwise the conversion tool will generate account numbers using GAAP methodology.
  • - Using sub accounts in quickbooks? In Xero sub accounts can be structured as 6000, 6000.10, 6000.20… or something similar.
  • - If you are using Jobs in Quickbooks Xero will create additional contacts for the jobs. Example is Joe:KitchenRemodel.
  • - When you receive your new Xero client company the bank accounts will need to be reconciled. This will complete the conversion process.

 

 

*Conversion Date: All transactions prior to this date will be condensed into one journal, and all transactions after

                                    this date will be converted.  The date MUST be the first or last day of a month.

 

** To provide a copy of your QuickBooks company file in .QBW format, do the following activities:

-        Open QuickBooks with your desired company file

-        Click “F2” to see the details of your company file

-        Determine the location of your company file under “File” and “Location”

-        Using that location, upload the company file (which would be the .QBW version) with this form to the Box account provided to you

 

** To provide a copy of your QuickBooks company file in .QBB format, do the following activities:

-        Open QuickBooks with your desired company file

-        Click “File” and then choose “Backup” from the menu

-        Determine the location of your company file under “File” and “Location”

-        Using that location, upload the company file (which would be the .QBB version) with this form to the Box account provided to you
